# Fintech Audit: Digital Transformation in German Banking

A comprehensive digital audit and business model redesign for the German Sparkassen-Finanzgruppe, integrating live data from Bundesbank, ECB, and Eurostat. Features:

- **Business Model Canvas**: Traditional vs. Neo-bank
- **KPI Dashboard**: Branch density, digital penetration, cost-to-income ratio
- **Data Pipeline**: Python ETL, Power BI analytics
- **Case Study**: DigiBIM methodology, digital pain points, and redesign strategy
