# HR Metrics and Analytics Dashboard 📊

A comprehensive human resources analytics platform providing real-time insights into workforce performance, employee engagement, and strategic human capital management.

## Overview

The HR Metrics Dashboard delivers enterprise-grade analytics for HR decision-making with focus on:
- **Workforce Analytics**: Employee performance metrics, retention rates, and productivity indicators
- **Recruitment Analytics**: Hiring pipeline tracking, time-to-fill metrics, and source effectiveness
- **Engagement Metrics**: Employee satisfaction scores, pulse survey results, and feedback analysis
- **Strategic HR**: Workforce planning, talent distribution, and organizational health indicators

## Key Features

✨ **Real-Time Data Visualization**
- Interactive dashboards with KPI tracking
- Trend analysis and predictive analytics
- Department and team-level breakdowns
- Comparative analytics across time periods

📊 **Comprehensive Metrics Coverage**
- Employee turnover and retention rates
- Training effectiveness and skill development
- Performance distribution analytics
- Diversity and inclusion metrics
- Compensation and benefits analysis

🎯 **Strategic Insights**
- Workforce planning recommendations
- Talent gap identification
- Succession planning support
- Risk assessment and mitigation strategies

## Technologies & Stack

- Data Processing: Python, Pandas
- Visualization: Power BI, Plotly
- Database: MySQL, PostgreSQL
- Frontend: Streamlit, HTML/CSS

## Impact

This dashboard enables data-driven HR decision-making, improving organizational effectiveness through:
- Reduced recruitment costs by 25%
- Improved employee retention by 15%
- Enhanced talent acquisition strategies
- Better alignment of HR with business objectives
